# Global Brand Guidelines

## Voice
- Confident, practical, and optimistic.
- Keep claims specific and avoid medical or absolute language.
- Focus on everyday utility and smart choices.

## Visual Rules
- Keep product as focal point.
- Ensure clean composition and strong foreground contrast.
- Preferred palette anchors:
  - #0057B8
  - #00A3E0
  - #0EA5E9

## Compliance Notes
- Avoid words: cure, guaranteed.
- Do not imply medical treatment outcomes.
