# Nike Brand Guidelines

## Voice
- Bold, inspiring, and energetic.
- Use language that motivates action and celebrates achievement.
- Emphasize innovation, performance, and possibility.
- Avoid medical, absolute, or unrealistic claims.

## Visual Rules
- Product and athlete should be the hero of every image.
- Dynamic, high-contrast compositions with movement and energy.
- Use Nike's iconic palette:
  - Black (#000000)
  - White (#FFFFFF)
  - Volt (#CEFF00)
  - University Red (#CD1041)
  - Nike Orange (#FF6600)
- Incorporate bold graphics, swoosh elements, and urban/sport settings.

## Compliance Notes
- Avoid words: cure, guaranteed, miracle, best-ever.
- Do not imply medical treatment or unattainable results.
- All claims must be substantiated and focus on real performance benefits.
